Institution's Innovation Council (IIC) & EKTA Incubation Centre - MAKAUT, WB

About IIC (ID: IC201810645)

The Institution's Innovation Council (IIC) at MAKAUT, established in **2018-19**, is dedicated to fostering an ecosystem of innovation and entrepreneurship. We aim to convert raw, innovative ideas from students, researchers, and faculty into socially relevant, market-ready products and services through mentorship and infrastructure support.

Vision & Mission

Vision

To create a robust ecosystem that encourages and enables innovations in contemporary and emerging technologies, driving efforts towards achieving Sustainable Development Goals.

Mission

  • Motivate the MAKAUT community to generate innovative ideas.
  • Provide expert mentoring and industry exposure.
  • Facilitate IPR protection (Patents, Design, Copyrights).
  • Offer access to specialized labs and infrastructure for prototype development.

EKTA Incubation Startup Showcase

A look at some of the successful ventures incubated and supported by the EKTA Incubation Centre (Representative Examples).

AgriSense Tech

AgriTech / IoT

Developed an affordable IoT-based sensor network for smart soil monitoring and automated irrigation, drastically improving crop yield for small farmers in rural Bengal.

Founders: Alumni of CSE Department, Batch 2020

Arogya Mitra

HealthTech / AI

A mobile application utilizing deep learning for preliminary diagnosis of common skin diseases through image analysis, bridging the healthcare gap in remote areas.

Founders: Students from Biotechnology & IT Departments

Green Fuel Solutions

Renewable Energy

Focuses on developing cost-effective and scalable methods for bio-fuel production from industrial and municipal waste, promoting sustainable energy alternatives.

Founders: Faculty & Researchers from Renewable Energy Dept.

EduVerse AI

EdTech / ML

A personalized learning platform that uses Machine Learning algorithms to track student progress and adapt curriculum difficulty in real-time for better engagement.

Founders: Alumni of Management & CSE Departments
