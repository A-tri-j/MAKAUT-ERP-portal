New Student Registration Guide

Academic Year 2025-2026

Welcome to the Registration Portal

This process is mandatory for all candidates who have been provisionally admitted to the university. Successful completion ensures your enrollment is confirmed, and you receive your official University Registration Number.

Step-by-Step Registration Process

  • Access the Portal: Log in to the official University Registration Portal using your Admission/Application ID and Date of Birth.
  • Fill the Registration Form: Complete all mandatory fields (Personal Details, Academic History, Contact Information, etc.) accurately.
  • Upload Documents: Scan and upload all required documents (see checklist below) in the specified format (JPEG/PDF) and size.
  • Pay Registration Fee: Pay the mandatory one-time registration fee and/or the remaining semester fees through the online payment gateway (Net Banking/Card/UPI). Print the receipt.
  • Verify and Submit: Review all entries and uploaded documents carefully. Once confirmed, submit the final application and download the 'Registration Acknowledgment Slip'.
  • Physical Verification (Offline): Report to the designated University department (Registrar/Admissions Office) on the specified date for physical verification of original documents.
  • Receive ID: Upon successful verification, collect your permanent University Registration Card and Student ID.

Mandatory Document Checklist (Scanned Copies)

  • Admission Allotment Letter (Counselling Letter).
  • Class 10th Marksheet and Certificate.
  • Class 12th Marksheet and Certificate.
  • Graduation/Diploma Marksheets (for PG courses).
  • Entrance Exam Scorecard (e.g., JEE/CAT/CET).
  • Caste/EWS Certificate (if applicable).
  • Aadhaar Card / Photo ID Proof.
  • Latest Passport-sized Photograph and Signature (as per specs).
  • Fee Payment Receipt (Online Transaction Slip).
