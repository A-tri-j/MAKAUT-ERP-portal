MAKAUT Admission Process 2024-25

Maulana Abul Kalam Azad University of Technology (MAKAUT), West Bengal

Key Courses and Entrance Exams

Admission to MAKAUT is primarily based on the scores of various national/state-level entrance exams or the CET/JEMAT/PGET conducted by the university.

Course Entrance Exam Brief Selection Process
**B.Tech** WBJEE / JEE Main Counselling based on WBJEE rank.
**MBA / MHA** JEMAT / CAT / MAT GD/PI based on JEMAT or other valid scores.
**MCA** JECA (WB JECA) Counselling based on JECA rank.
**BCA / BBA / B.Sc / M.Sc** CET (MAKAUT Common Entrance Test) Merit based on CET score.
**M.Tech / M.Pharm** GATE / PGET (MAKAUT) Merit based on GATE/PGET score.
**B.Tech (Lateral Entry)** JELET Counselling based on JELET rank.

General Admission Steps

  • Apply for Entrance Exam: Apply online for the relevant entrance exam (WBJEE, JEMAT, CET, etc.) and pay the application fee.
  • Appear for the Exam: Download the admit card and appear for the entrance examination on the scheduled date.
  • Result and Merit List: Check your rank/score after the result declaration. A merit list will be published based on the scores.
  • Counselling/Interview (GD/PI): Shortlisted candidates must participate in the counselling process or Group Discussion/Personal Interview (GD/PI).
  • Seat Allotment & Verification: Report to the allotted institution with all necessary documents for verification upon receiving the seat allotment letter.
  • Admission Fee Payment: Pay the final admission fee after successful document verification to confirm your seat.

Required Documents (for Verification)

  • Entrance Exam Admit Card and Scorecard.
  • Class 10 Pass Certificate / Admit Card.
  • Class 12 Mark Sheet and Pass Certificate.
  • Graduation/Diploma Mark Sheet and Degree (for PG Courses).
  • Aadhaar Card and Passport-sized Photographs.
  • Caste / Domicile Certificate (if applicable).
  • Seat Allotment Letter.
**Important Note:** All candidates must regularly check the **Official MAKAUT Website (www.makautwb.ac.in)** for the latest notifications. The admission process and dates are subject to change.
