Research Overview

Maulana Abul Kalam Azad University of Technology (MAKAUT) encourages cutting-edge research across Engineering, Science, Technology, and Management domains. The research cell facilitates grants, collaborations, and publication activities for faculty and students, focusing on impactful, interdisciplinary projects with societal relevance.
