About Us

Maulana Abul Kalam Azad University of Technology (MAKAUT), West Bengal, formerly known as WBUT, was established in 2001 by the Government of West Bengal. Named after India‚Äôs first Education Minister, it is a state university offering education and research in Engineering, Technology, Management, Science, and Applied Arts. With its main campus at Haringhata, Nadia, MAKAUT affiliates over 200 institutions across the state and is committed to promoting innovation, research, and skill-based education.

Premier State University for technical, professional, and management education in West Bengal. Affiliates 200+ colleges and institutes across the state. Offers UG, PG, and PhD programs in Engineering, Science, Management, and Applied Arts. Focus on research, innovation, and industry collaboration. Committed to skill development and entrepreneurship for students. Ensures quality education aligned with national and global standards.

Academics

At MAKAUT University, we offer a wide range of undergraduate and postgraduate programs designed to equip students with the knowledge and skills needed for success in their chosen fields. Our academic curriculum is continuously updated to reflect the latest industry trends and technological advancements, ensuring that our graduates are well-prepared to meet the demands of the job market.

Program

Our engineering programs are designed to provide students with a strong foundation in technical skills and problem-solving abilities, preparing them for careers in various engineering fields.

Faculty

Our faculty members are experts in their fields, bringing a wealth of knowledge and experience to the classroom. They are dedicated to providing students with a supportive and engaging learning environment.

Research

We encourage students to engage in research activities, providing opportunities to work on innovative projects and collaborate with faculty on cutting-edge research initiatives.

Facilities

Our campus is equipped with state-of-the-art facilities, including modern classrooms, laboratories, libraries, and recreational areas.

Extracurricular Activities

We believe in a holistic approach to education, encouraging students to participate in various extracurricular activities, clubs, and organizations.

Internship and Placement

We have strong industry connections that facilitate internship and placement opportunities. Our placement cell works with top companies to ensure our students are job-ready.

Departments

MAKAUT University offers a diverse range of departments, each dedicated to providing specialized education and training in various fields. Our departments are staffed by experienced faculty members who are committed to fostering a supportive and engaging learning environment for all students.

Computer Science Engineering

The Engineering Department offers programs in various engineering disciplines, including Computer Science, Mechanical Engineering, and Civil Engineering.

VLSI Engineering

The Electrical Engineering Department focuses on the study of electrical systems, electronics, and electromagnetism, preparing students for careers in various industries.

Information & Technology Engineering Department

The Information & Technology Engineering Department provides students with the skills and knowledge needed to excel in the rapidly evolving field of information technology.

Campus Life

At MAKAUT University, we believe that a vibrant campus life is essential for a well-rounded education. Our campus offers a wide range of activities and facilities to enhance the student experience.

Sports and Fitness

Our campus features state-of-the-art sports facilities, including a gymnasium, sports fields, and courts for various sports. We encourage students to participate in sports and fitness activities to maintain a healthy lifestyle.

Library and Study Spaces

Our library is equipped with a vast collection of books, journals, and digital resources. We also provide quiet study spaces for students to focus on their academic work.

Hostel and Accommodation

We offer comfortable and affordable hostel facilities for students, ensuring a safe and conducive living environment. Our hostels are equipped with modern amenities to support students' needs.

Cultural Events

Our campus hosts a variety of cultural events throughout the year, including music festivals, art exhibitions, and theater performances. These events provide students with opportunities to showcase their talents and engage with the wider community.
